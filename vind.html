• What is Vindinium?

    • Vindinium is a game where you make your own AI bot and you have a goal of to collect as much gold as you can and whoever has the most gold wins. It is a 4 player battle where you face other people and what you can do is capture mines owned by goblins which are called free mines, attack enemy mines where other players captures free mines, attack other bots or you can do all of that if you program your bot well enough.

  • What can my bot do?

    • Right now, what my bot can do is that it can collect free mines, enemy mines and it can go to the taverns for health.

  • When does my bot do these things?

    • First of all, when the game starts, my bot starts to go capture as many free mines as it can until there is no more left. If the bots hp is 35 or less, then the bot starts to head to the closest tavern and repeats it everytime it is either at that amount of hp or less. Then when there is no more free mines, it starts going for enemy mines. If an enemy walks in the bots way, them the bots attack each other.

  • What do I think the best strategy is?

    • I believe the best strategy is once you get quite a bit of mines, then go camp around a tavern and kills anyone who comes near the tavern. Since I can't really do that because I don't know how to do those yet, then I believe the strategy I can use is to capture free and enemy mines and kill any other bots that are around so I can capture more mines and get more gold.
